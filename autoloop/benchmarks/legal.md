# Legal Benchmarks / 法律行业基准测试

## Task 1: 合同审查
Given a commercial contract (NDA, SaaS agreement, or licensing deal):
- Identify non-standard or unusual clauses
- Flag risk allocation issues (indemnification, liability caps, IP ownership)
- Compare key terms with market standard
- Suggest specific redline edits with rationale
- Summarize in a deal-point checklist

评估标准: 风险条款识别率、市场标准判断准确度、修改建议的专业性

## Task 2: 案例研究与判例检索
Given a legal issue and jurisdiction:
- Identify relevant statutes and regulations
- Find analogous case precedents
- Analyze how precedents apply to current facts
- Identify distinguishing factors
- Draft preliminary legal analysis memo

评估标准: 法条引用准确性、判例相关度、分析逻辑严密性

## Task 3: 尽职调查报告
Given a target company's corporate documents:
- Identify corporate structure and ownership chain
- Flag governance issues (board composition, voting rights)
- Review material contracts and commitments
- Identify pending or potential litigation
- Summarize key findings with risk ratings

评估标准: 问题识别完整度、风险评级合理性、遗漏率
